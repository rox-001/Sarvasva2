Educational Visit To Infantry Museum

Forensic Science By Aditya Suman, Semester IV

The Department of Forensic Science at Government Holkar Science College, Indore, led by Dr. Geetha Sarasan, organized an educational visit to the Infantry Museum, Mhow,...
....to strengthen experiential learning and connect classroom theory with real‑world military history. Students explored India’s first dedicated military museum—17 themed rooms with 3D models, murals, archival records, regiment galleries, the Kargil War room, immersive dioramas, outdoor tanks and vehicles, and a 106‑foot national flag—gaining insights into documentation, preservation, research, and the evolution of the Indian Infantry.

Certificate Course

Botany By Prof. Sameera, Faculty of Botany

A six‑month certificate course in mushroom cultivation By Department of Botany...
...offers a skill‑based, entrepreneurship‑oriented program combining mycology theory with intensive hands‑on training. It covers cultivation of Button, Oyster, and Milky mushrooms, spawn production, substrate preparation, pest and disease management, post‑harvest technology, value addition, and marketing. Practical sessions include composting, low‑cost unit setup, harvesting, grading, packaging, and farm visits; assessment is via internal evaluation and a project, promoting self‑employment and sustainable income..
